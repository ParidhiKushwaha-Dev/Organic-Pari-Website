Awareness Guide: Injected vs Natural

In Jabalpur's local markets, many vendors use chemicals to make vegetables look fresh and green. Here is how you can spot the difference.

  • Excessive Greenery: If a vegetables like Spinach or Bottle Gourd look unnaturally Bright Green, it might be dyed with Malachit Green
  • Injection Marks: Look for tiny holes or dark spots near the stem. These are often signs of Oxytocin Injection used to grow them overnight
  • Shelf Life:Organic vegetables with naturally, while chemically treated ones might stay stiff but rot from the inside quickly

Stay safe and choose farm-fresh produce for your family's health.
